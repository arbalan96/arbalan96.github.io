Hi! I am a Ph.D. student at the Technical University of Munich, supervised by Prof. Javier Esparza and Prof. Igor Walukiewicz.

My research interests include parameterized verification, automata theory and well-quasi orders.
You can find more about my research on [dblp](https://dblp.org/pid/215/5409.html).
